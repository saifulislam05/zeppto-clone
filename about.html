Zeepto story

Zeppto was created in 2019 by Gregory Zerbib (CEO) and Frederic Minot (CTO) with the main goal of helping e-commerces boost their mobile conversion rates with 1-click checkouts that are easy to activate on any e-commerce platform, with any payment service provider.

The Zeepto founders

CEO

Gregory Zerbib

Gregory worked for over 8 years in sales and product management at Google and 4 years in data analysis at BCG. He lived and worked in diverse countries across the world before moving back to his home-town of Paris to co-found Zeppto with ex-classmate Frederic. In his spare time Gregory likes to fly his plane.

CTO

Frederic Minot

Frederic is a serial startup entrepreneur who has founded and developed several startups, with Zeppto being his most ambitious one yet. He is specialised in solution architecture and IT operations. In his spare time Frederic likes to code.
