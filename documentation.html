Technical Documentation

Zeppto does not require you to undertake any API
integration nor SDK installation for most features. Our
solution is instead:

Easy to activate on any content management system

Zeppto's unique solution is based on a JavaScript Library (a 'tag') that is customised to your web environment by our developers. To activate our solution simply add this tag to all pages of your website. See detailed instructions for installing our tag here.

Easy to activate with your payment service provider

Zeppto is not a payment service provider. Zeppto's solution is compatible with all of the leading payment service providers available on the market.

Adapted to your processes

All orders facilitated by Zeppto are technically processed and confirmed through your regular checkout: authorisation, capturing and accounting stay untouched.

Customised to your checkout

Our solution is customised to your website by Zeppto developers, and not the contrary, so you do not have to code anything.
