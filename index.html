Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zeppto a payment service provider?

Zeppto is not a payment service provider. Zeppto provides a solution that activates 1-click checkouts on your e-commerce with your payment service provider.

How do refunds work?

All your payment flows will go through your payment service provider. Same reconciliation, accounting, reimbursement processes.

Which languages and currencies does Zeppto support?

Zeppto supports all languages and all currencies. Contact us for your specific needs.

Is Zeppto compatible with installement payments?

Zeppto supports all installement payments and shop now, pay later payment methods available on the market (Klarna, Sofort, Afterpay, Affirm, Alma, 4X PayPal, etc.)

Is Zeppto compatible with package pick-up points?

Zeppto is compatible with all delivery methods, including package pick-up points.

Does Zeppto affect website performance?

Zeppto's tag can be loaded asynchronously and therefore does not affect the performance of your website.
